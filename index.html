About Me

I am a results-driven Applications Development Manager with a strong foundation in intelligent automation, cloud technology, and digital transformation. With dual undergraduate degrees in Computer Science and Marketing, and a graduate degree in Computer Science, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and business acumen to every project I undertake. Throughout my career at Genworth, I've advanced from roles in IT security and support to specializing in intelligent automation and application development. I am dedicated to delivering impactful solutions that meet and exceed organizational objectives, always looking to innovate and optimize processes.
